About Us

A Trusted Partner in Private Capital

Founded in Helsinki, Nalle Capital Oy is a privately held investment company with deep roots in the Nordic financial landscape. We partner with exceptional founders and management teams to build businesses of enduring quality.

Our approach combines rigorous financial analysis with a genuine commitment to the companies we back — providing not just capital, but strategic guidance and a long-term perspective.
